About the Decision Rule

⅓ threshold — reteach or advance?

If more than ⅓ of exit tickets are "Not Yet": open tomorrow with a 10-minute whole-class reteach for that group (while the rest start on an extension problem), then continue.

If ≤ ⅓ are "Not Yet": move the class forward. Pull the "Not Yet" group to a small reteach table during tomorrow's Explore. Add this concept to the spiral Do-Now for the next two weeks.

"Almost" pile: These students usually just need one more example or a chance to ask a question. They can often be addressed during tomorrow's Launch with a targeted question rather than a full reteach.
Notes
The ⅓ decision rule is a practical heuristic, not a hard cutoff — adjust based on the importance of the concept to upcoming content.
