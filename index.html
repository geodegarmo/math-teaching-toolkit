The Sticky Math Block — your house structure

Every period runs the same five-phase structure: Spiral Do-Now → Launch → Explore → Discuss & Lock-In → Exit Ticket. It's the NC2ML Launch–Explore–Discuss model with retrieval practice bolted on front and formative data bolted on back. Once students know the rhythm, the structure itself carries cognitive load.

Time budget: Do-Now 5–8 min · Launch 5 min · Explore 20–25 min · Discuss 10–12 min · Exit Ticket 3–5 min = 43–55 min total.
The Block Timer tool runs a countdown for each phase and advances automatically.
